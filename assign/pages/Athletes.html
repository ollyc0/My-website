Michael Jordan Signs with Nike

In 1984, Michael Jordan signed a contract with Nike that would change the landscape of sports marketing forever. The deal, initially worth $500,000 a year for five years, eventually led to the creation of the Air Jordan brand.

Michael Jordan's partnership with Nike revolutionized the sneaker industry, and Air Jordans became one of the most iconic and sought-after athletic shoe lines in history.

Today, the Air Jordan brand continues to thrive, with new releases generating excitement and demand around the world.

Michael Jordan's collaboration with Nike not only transformed him into a global sports icon but also paved the way for other athletes to establish lucrative endorsement deals.
